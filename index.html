Gogodo is a freelancing app, that enables the clients and the freelancers to communicate with each other with ease and you can pay the freelancers through the app itself.
When compared to other platforms, it's easy for freelancers to get registered and you'd be having no issue in communicating/negotiating.

Why choose GOGODO?

Clients don't have to wait till the freelancer accepts your invitation(since there's nothing called as invitation!). you can directly look at their profile and it's your choice to decide.

Clients can discuss with their respective freelancers about the payment strategy(either you can pay the initial amount/you can pay the freelancer after your job gets completed/pay them beforehand )
